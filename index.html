About me

My name is Joshua Odoemenam, I go by the alias O'shea Jacob. I am from the eastern part of Nigeria. I provide digital painting servives using Autodesk Sketchbook. I have been a freelance digital painter for over 2 years now working with Organizations like SpringAct. I am an art enthusiast who dedicates his time and effort creating a well detailed painting. I have arranged my projects/works for you in albums to help you navigate easily and have a quick look. Do reach out to me via any of my contacts displayed at the end of the page or via my socials to book my services or leave feedbacks. I cannot wait to work with you.
